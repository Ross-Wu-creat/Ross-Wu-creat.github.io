About me

Hanyi Wu is an interdisciplinary data scientist and geographer interested in health risk and inequality in the changing environment, crop mapping, timeseries satellite data reconstruction, and soil erosion. He developed remote sensing approaches combined with machine learning in order to characterize forms of cities not only at large scales but also a spatially-explicit resolution close to people’s living environment to understand health.
