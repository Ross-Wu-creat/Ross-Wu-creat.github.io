About me

Hanyi Wu (å´ç€šé€¸) is currently pursuing his PhD degree at Tsinghua University. He is interested in integrating the AI & RS for climate adaptation, espectially the health risk assessment and environmental & agricultural remote sensing. He developed remote sensing approaches combined with machine learning in order to characterize patterns of cities not only at large scales but also a spatially-explicit resolution close to people's living environment to understand health.
